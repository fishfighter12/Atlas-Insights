Common questions

How do I add or remove charts?

Use the Build Report tab: search a ticker, pick its chart style and timeframe, and add it to your Daily or Weekly briefing. Remove charts from the lists at the bottom of the same tab, or with the remove button on any chart card.

When do my charts update?

At your chosen refresh time (Account tab → Daily report refresh). Open the app any time after that and the briefing is already assembled.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple: Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Morning Brief. Cancelling never deletes your saved charts.

How do I delete my data?

All data lives on your device. Account tab → Reset App Data erases everything, or simply delete the app.
