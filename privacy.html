Privacy Policy

Atlas Insights · Effective July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer of Atlas Insights ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the Atlas Insights mobile application and the atlasinsightsapp.com website (together, the "Service"). It forms part of our Terms of Service.

The short version: your data stays on your device. Atlas Insights has no accounts, no sign-in, and no server of ours that receives your information. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information, and the app contains no advertising or analytics SDKs. The only data that leaves your device are the anonymous market data requests needed to draw your charts and the purchase processing Apple performs for subscriptions.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

None. The Service has no accounts, profiles, or sign-in, and no forms that submit information to us. Everything you configure in the app (your chart lineup, chart and display settings, and refresh schedule) is stored only on your device. If you email us for support, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include, and we use it only to respond.

Information collected automatically

We run no servers and operate no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, so we do not automatically collect usage data, identifiers, or location. To draw charts, the app requests price history for the ticker symbols you follow directly from third-party market data providers. Those requests necessarily include the ticker symbols requested and your device's IP address, as any internet request does, but include no name, account, identifier, or other information about you. Each provider's handling of requests is governed by its own privacy policy: Twelve Data, Alpha Vantage, Yahoo.

Information from third parties

We receive none, with one narrow exception: if you purchase a subscription, we receive anonymous subscription status information (not your identity or payment details) as described in Section 3.

2. How We Use Information

The information described above is used solely to provide the Service: on-device data makes the app work, market data requests draw your charts, and subscription status unlocks Pro features. We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, cross-device tracking, or marketing, and we send no marketing communications.

3. Purchases (Atlas Insights Pro)

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your App Store account; we never receive your name, payment details, or billing address. The app may use the Superwall SDK to present the upgrade screen and manage subscription status. Superwall receives anonymized device and subscription status information for this purpose and does not use it to track you across other companies' apps or websites. See Superwall's privacy policy.

4. How We Disclose Information

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose personal information, because we hold none to disclose. The only parties that process anything in connection with the Service are Apple (purchases), the market data providers (chart requests, as described above), and Superwall (anonymous subscription status), each under its own policy. If we were ever legally compelled to disclose information, there would be nothing in our possession to produce beyond support emails you have sent us.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your configuration and cached market data live only on your device, and you control them completely: Account → Reset App Data permanently erases everything the app has stored, as does deleting the app. We retain support emails only as long as needed to resolve your request. Because we hold no personal data, there is nothing further for us to retain or delete.

6. Security

On-device data is protected by iOS's built-in protections, including encryption of files at rest. Market data requests are made over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but keeping your data on your device and collecting nothing removes the central point of failure a server-side breach would represent.

7. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has sent us personal information by email, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Use

We are based in the United States, and the third-party providers the app contacts are U.S.-based services. If you use the Service from outside the United States, your market data requests are processed in the United States. No other personal information is transferred, because none is collected.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Privacy laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the EU/UK GDPR, and similar state and national laws grant rights to access, correct, delete, port, and restrict the processing of personal data, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising them. We honor these rights by design: we do not collect or hold your personal data, so there is nothing for us to produce, correct, or restrict, and nothing is ever "sold" or "shared" as those laws define. Your data is in your hands on your device: accessing it is using the app, and deleting it is Reset App Data. If you believe we hold personal information about you (for example, past support correspondence), email support@atlasinsightsapp.com and we will respond within the time required by applicable law, and you may appeal any decision by replying to our response. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

10. Changes to This Policy

If our practices change (for example, if a future version of the app adds accounts or a backend service), we will update this policy, post the new version at this address with a new effective date, and provide reasonable notice of material changes in the app before they take effect.
